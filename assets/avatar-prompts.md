# Board of Directors Avatar Prompts for Gemini

Style guide: Oil painting portrait, classical realism, warm amber lighting from the left, marble column and leather chair barely visible in background, dark neutral backdrop. Chest-up framing. Same artistic style as the council banquet table paintings. Each figure should be recognizable but stylized as a painted portrait, not photorealistic. Square aspect ratio (1:1).

---

## Generic Template (for new agents)

Copy this and fill in the bracketed fields:

```
Oil painting portrait in classical realism style, warm amber lighting, dark neutral background with faint marble columns, chest-up framing, square 1:1 aspect ratio. [FULL NAME], [AGE DESCRIPTION], [PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION: hair, face, build], wearing [CLOTHING DESCRIPTION]. Expression: [EXPRESSION AND MOOD], [METAPHORICAL DESCRIPTION OF THEIR GAZE OR ENERGY]. [OPTIONAL: one small prop or detail visible near the edge of frame].
```

**Field guide:**
- **FULL NAME**: As they'd be recognized (e.g., "Admiral James Stavridis" not just "Stavridis")
- **AGE DESCRIPTION**: Era-appropriate age (e.g., "mid-50s", "late 60s")
- **PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION**: 2-3 distinguishing features (hair, glasses, build)
- **CLOTHING DESCRIPTION**: Signature outfit or era-appropriate attire
- **EXPRESSION**: Emotional quality + intellectual quality (e.g., "skeptical intensity")
- **METAPHORICAL DESCRIPTION**: What their look conveys about who they are
- **OPTIONAL PROP**: Something associated with them, barely visible at frame edge
