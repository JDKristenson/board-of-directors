## Post-Judgment Options

If you wish to continue deliberation:
- **Challenge the judgment**: "Marcus, I believe [specific point] is wrong because..."
- **Request elaboration**: "Can you explain your reasoning on [specific aspect]?"
- **Introduce new information**: "What if I told you that [new fact]?"
- **Request agent follow-up**: "Can [Agent X] respond to your judgment?"
