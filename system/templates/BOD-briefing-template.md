# Council Briefing Template

Use this template to structure context for the Board of Directors.

---

## Quick Briefing (Minimum Viable Context)

```markdown
# Council Briefing — [TOPIC]

## The Decision
[One sentence: What decision are you facing?]

## Options
1. [Option A]
2. [Option B]
3. [Do nothing / status quo]

## Stakes
- **If right**: [Best realistic outcome]
- **If wrong**: [Worst realistic outcome]
- **Timeline**: [When must be decided]

## The Real Question
[What's the deeper question beneath the surface question?]
```

## Usage Notes

1. **Start with the minimum viable briefing** and add detail if needed
2. **Be honest in the subtext section** — agents can give better advice when they know your biases
3. **Name the deeper question** — often more important than the surface question
4. **Attach relevant documents** — council can reference specific data
5. **Don't overthink** — some detail is better than paralysis
